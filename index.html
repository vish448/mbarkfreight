About Us

Mbark Freight Inc. is a cargo and freight forwarding company who offload your freight & do your customs requirements in Canada. We import and export goods anywhere in the world.

Services

What We Do.

Clear and accurate information is our goal. We will take care of it all for you!

Freight Forwarding & Project Cargo

Air, Sea, and Road are the three most common types of freight transportaion. We make sure the whatever medium you choose with us, you will expect compitive quotes and services from us. Project cargo is special type of freight shipping that suggests moving high-volume and high-price freight.

Customs Broker

We handle customs process for shipments. whether they go by air, ocean, or ground. These services include, but are not limited to: Completing required documentation for export and import shipments. Clearing shipments of imported goods.

Warehouse Distribution

We also handle warehouse distribution of your goods and making the goods available for consumption by business users and final consumers. A combination of warehousing involves the acquisition of goods from manufactures, storage of the goods and availing the goods to the consumers.

Inland Transportation

We handle all ground and rail transport activities. And we make sure that your goods are in same exact origin shape from the time of pick to the delivery.
